About Us

Welcome to Fairy Delivery – the magical solution for instant and seamless deliveries! Unlike traditional services, we operate without drop-off or pick-up points. Your parcels teleport directly from sender to recipient, hand-to-hand, with the help of our enchanting fairy technology. All we need from you are the precise coordinates of both sender and receiver. Experience a new era of delivery magic!

How to Use Our Site

  1. Create a Delivery: Navigate to the "Delivery" section to initiate a new delivery. Enter the sender's and receiver's details, including their coordinates, and provide information about the parcel.
  2. Track Your Delivery: Go to the "Tracking" section to monitor your parcel in real-time. Use the unique delivery code to check its status.
  3. Calculate Price: Visit the "Calculate Price" section to estimate the delivery cost based on the parcel's size, weight, and distance.

Join us in making delivery stress-free and magical. Fairy Delivery – where every delivery is a fairy tale come true!
